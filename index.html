About

Ricky Christie is a cinematographer and director of photography whose work spans narrative film, commercial, and music video. His camera work earned two Gold Summit Awards for the Ontario Hunter Jumper Association film.

His Toronto 48 Hour Film Project entry won the city competition and went on to screen at Filmapalooza in Lisbon.

He also directs — including music videos produced through Hifi Sound Portraits — and partners with directors, agencies, and artists to shape image and light.
